ADnD Campaign House Rules

Primary Books

  • Players Handbook (PH)
  • Unearthed Arcana (UA).

DnD Classes

  • Fighter
  • Ranger
  • Barbarian
  • Cavalier (Lawful Only) (UA)
  • Paladin (Lawful Good Deity only)
  • Paladin (All other alignments Dragon Magazine #106)
  • Thief
  • Thief-Acrobat (UA)
  • Assassin (Evil or Neutral Only)
  • Magic User
  • Illusionist
  • Witch (Dragon Magazine #114)
  • Cleric (Deity required)
  • Druid
  • Monk
  • Bard (Dragon Magazine #56)
  • We also can choose  most classes from Dragon Magazine.

Race:

This realm is primarily:

  • human 50%
  • halfling 20%
  • Dwarves 15%
  • Elves 10%
  • Other 5%  (Half-Orcs and Gnomes)

Alignment:

  • Any (See PH P.33 for descriptions and Dragon #60)

Magic

  • Magic is rare and mysterious. Magic users are not trusted and feared by the middle and lower classes.

Legal Spells

  • All Listed spells in PH and UA.
  • All ADnD spells in Dragon Magazine articles
  • 2E Spells in Dragon Magazine (per approval)
  • All other edition spells (per approval and conversion)
  • Spell book guidelines (Dragon Magazine #62)

ERRATA:

  • MM, PH & DMG errata (dragon #35)
  • UA errata (dragon #103)

Ability Score Training

  • As Cavalier (UA p.15)
  • Other classes only improve one ability score. Character choice of the ability score.

Notable Additions

  • Thieves tools #115
  • Critical hits/fumbles system (Dragon Magazine #39 p. 34-35)
  • Spell misfires (purpleworm.org Google Doc)

  • Infravision (2E DMG p.118, also explained in the blog)
